(tl;dr résumé)

Hello! My name is Thomas. I am a human with a passion for design and open source technology currently based in Chicago, IL. I am currently a Software Development Engineer at 4C Insights. I graduated from Loyola University Chicago with a B.S. in Computer Science in May 2016.

As a Software Development Engineer at 4C, I work on a large one-page SaaS ads management tool for ad activation in premium marketplaces. Currently I am helping to spearhead an effort to transition our full frontend codebase to React from Ember.js by creating a solid reusable component library for cross-platform usage. I am part of a small team that planned, built and maintains a cross-publisher audience creation and management tool. Users can target, build, upload and expansion model audiences based on different inputs to use with our social and TV integrations. I work with multiple third-party API integrations as well as with 4C's own in-house data for ad audience building, data manipulation and publishing. Additionally, I have had the opportunity to help lead and maintain the building and design of a high-level data visualization tool that makes use of TV and social data to help brands better understand their impact and audience insights in addition to how they compare to their competitors. I built a chat-ops bot to help the team monitor the status of our continuous integration tests and tools pipeline. I also mentor new junior engineers and often help evaluate engineering candidates.

In the processing of building and working at 4C, I've gained experience working with a full stack of languages such as JavaScript (ES6), Python, HTML, CSS/LESS, Node.js and technologies such as React, Ember.js, Flask, MySQL, MongoDB, Redis, Redux, Immutable.js, AWS, SQLAlchemy, Pandas and Celery.

I spend my free time researching and exploring the confluence of technology, design and human rights. And possibly decentralized technologies. I love to cook/bake, travel and read (currently Homo Deus: A Brief History of Tomorrow). I also enjoy photography, cartography, running, biking and hiking.

You can find me on GitHub, Twitter, and LinkedIn.
